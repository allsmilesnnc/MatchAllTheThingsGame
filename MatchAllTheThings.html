Instructions

Before you begin, be sure to select the difficulty setting by adjusting the Game Level down(-) or up(+). 0 for example will allow the pictures, after selected, to remain on the screen longer where a higher setting will perform the opposite. The highest difficulty level is 9. These options can only be changed after the game has stopped.

To start the game, click on the 'Start' button.

The game will track how many matches were found, the number of attempts it took to make matches, and the total time in seconds occurred while the game is in play. You can stop the game at any time by clicking on the red'Stop' button.

After you complete the game, you will be asked to enter in your Name so you can be included on the High Score list. Next, be sure to press the High Score button to see your results. You can play as many times as you like or take turns with someone else. The scores will be ranked and displayed on the High Score list. Have Fun! Suggestion: After each game, increase the game level. You may be surprised how fast you can make matches!
